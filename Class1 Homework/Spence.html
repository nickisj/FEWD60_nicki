ABOUT ME

As an intellectual property ("IP") attorney for more than six years, I have gained experience in litigation, commercial transactions, enforcement and prosecution. My passion for IP is driven by the fact that I am a technology enthusiast. From the life sciences to software design, I have always been interested in cutting edge technology, scientific innovation, and I strongly believe that technology, business methods, and innovation are excellent mechanisms for social change and making a difference. It is that passion which has led me to take a Front End Web Development course at General Assembly.

During my spare time, I love baking (especially cupcakes), reading, traveling (especially to my native country of Jamaica)! I am also a foodie who loves to explore new restaurants, as well as explore the amazing borough of Queens!
